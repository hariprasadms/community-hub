üí° Pro Tips for Using Apps in UK

  • Set up UK App Store account: Some apps only available in UK store
  • Enable location services: Many transport and food apps need this
  • Notifications: Enable for banking, NHS, and delivery apps
  • WiFi first: Download large apps on WiFi to save mobile data
  • Offline maps: Download Google Maps offline for your area
  • Student discounts: Many apps offer student pricing (Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Prime)
  • Cashback apps: TopCashback, Quidco for money back on purchases
  • Storage management: Regularly clear cache and delete unused apps
  • Privacy settings: Review app permissions regularly
  • Auto-updates: Keep apps updated for security and features
