üí° Smart Shopping Strategy

  1. Monthly bulk buy: Rice, lentils, flour from Cash & Carry (saves 30-40%)
  2. Weekly grocery: Fresh vegetables from Indian store on Saturday
  3. Quick top-ups: Tesco/Sainsbury's for convenience + Clubcard points
  4. Special items: Order online when you need specific regional products
  5. Ready meals: Cook in bulk and freeze (cheaper than ready meals)
